Assignment 3

Our third assignment was to research more on the final project that I intend to build. I first went to websites like Instructables and Thingyverse to look as projects that people have done and build mine around one.

I chose to make a door sensor. This door sensor will track when the door is moving. It could also detect if the door is being opened or if someone is closing the door.

The sensor revolves around this magnetic switch that sends a signal to Arduino that the door is moving.
