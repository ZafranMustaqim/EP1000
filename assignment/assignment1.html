Assignment 1

To start making the phone stand, I first had to create a sketch of what I intend the prototype to look like.
After sketching, I cut out a piece of cardboard and folded it in half.
I then sketched the profile of the stand so that I can cut out the stand according to the profile that I drew on the cardboard.
Finally I cut out the phone stand and put my phone on it to show that it can support the weight of my phone without jeopardising too much of the display.

The phone stand that I created could use a lot of refining to make it look neater. It is not that portable even though it can fold as it was still pretty big. It could have folded a bit more to make it more portable. The profile of the stand could also be cut out better to make it more presentable. I think that the problem could have been due to me lacking cardboard. This means that I had lesser cardboard to work with.
